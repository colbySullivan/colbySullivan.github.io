TGP I

Project Description

TGP I was my first major team-based game development project, where I collaborated with 3 fellow students to create a complete gaming experience. This project emphasized communication, task delegation, and integrated workflows.

Working as part of a development team, I contributed to game design, programming implementation, and testing phases. The project taught valuable lessons in version control, collaborative problem-solving, and the importance of clear documentation. Our team successfully delivered a polished prototype that showcased our collective skills and creative vision.

Fallen

Project Description

Fallen was developed during a game jam where I served as the sole programmer on a team of four, working alongside two artists and a producer. This fast-paced development experience required rapid prototyping, efficient communication, and quick problem-solving under tight time constraints.

As the only programmer, I was responsible for implementing all gameplay mechanics, integrating art assets, and ensuring technical functionality while collaborating closely with the artists to bring their vision to life. The producer helped coordinate our efforts and kept the team on track. This project demonstrated my ability to work effectively in cross-disciplinary teams and deliver functional code under pressure while maintaining clear communication with non-technical team members.
